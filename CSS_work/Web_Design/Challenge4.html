Motivation

Needs

1. Desires , wants or drives within the individuals.

2. It is the process of stimulating people to actions to accomplish the goals.

Two Basic Motivators

1. Desire to obtain or get something.

2. Fear of losing something

Tips to increase Motivation

  • Hang Around with Positive, Motivating People.
  • Post a picture of your goal someplace visible - Visualizing your goal, exactly how you think it will be when you’ve achieved it.
  • Find inspiration, on a daily basis- Inspiration is one of the best motivators, and it can be found everywhere. Sources of inspiration can include: blogs, online success stories, quotes, music, photos, people you meet.
  • Found powerful reasons- What you actually want- Write them down in dairy. Know your reasons. Give them some thought… If you have loved ones, and you are doing it , that is more powerful.
  • Make a big public commitment- Be fully committed. This will do the trick every time. Create a blog and announce to the world that you are going to achieve a certain goal by a certain date. Commit yourself to the hilt.
  • Always think positive- Monitor your thoughts. If you hear negative thoughts, stop them, push them out, and replace them with positive thoughts. Positive thinking can be amazingly powerful.
  • Learn to Set and Reach Goals- Research has shown that one of the best ways to create motivation is to learn how to set goals and then reach them.
  • Reward yourself- I talked about breaking larger goals into smaller, mini goals. Well, each of those mini goals should have a reward attached to it....
